# Orchestra Design System

<Text fontFamily="ff-mulish" fontSize="fs-3"><Text as="strong" fontFamily="ff-mulish" fontWeight="fw-bold" fontSize="fs-3">Orchestra</Text> is a Design System created by <Anchor href="https://yannisabel.com" title="Yann Isabel's website" isExternal>Yann Isabel</Anchor> for his own purpose. It aims to follow guidelines inspired by the music area.</Text>

<Text fontFamily="ff-mulish" fontSize="fs-3" className="margin-bottom--space-5">The component library called <Text as="strong" fontFamily="ff-mulish" fontWeight="fw-bold" fontSize="fs-3">Orchestra Kit</Text> follows the Orchestra principles and allows to create web interfaces with flexibility.</Text>

## The story behind Orchestra

Yann Isabel is a musician since he was teenagers so it was natural to think about a music correlation with his Design System.

Music is a language with multiple parts in it. From the notation to how the music is played or how it sounds there are plenty of rules and guidelines to follow to call it music and not only sound.
